Privacy Policy — TheraPaletteStudio Pinterest Integration

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This application ("the App") is a private, single-business integration built and operated by TheraPaletteStudio to publish Pins to its own Pinterest business account (prmptpltt) programmatically, replacing manual/third-party scheduling.

What the App does

The App uses the Pinterest API (v5) to create Pins and Boards on the TheraPaletteStudio Pinterest business account on our behalf. It does not act on behalf of any other Pinterest user or account.

Data we access

Through Pinterest's OAuth flow, the App requests only the scopes needed to read and create Pins/Boards on our own account (e.g. pins:read, pins:write, boards:read, boards:write). We do not collect, store, or process personal data belonging to any Pinterest user other than the authenticated business account owner (Harrison Watts).

Data storage

OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored securely and used solely to authenticate API calls from this integration. We do not sell, share, or use this data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose outside operating our own Pinterest account. No end-user data is collected because the App has no end users other than the account owner.
